Solar System Express Interview

By: Michael D Mooring

I had the pleasure to interview Blaze, Paul, and Kunal of Sol-X where we talked about the DIY Rocket Contest which Sol-X is part of. The contest is to design a open-source 3D printed, steel (in Sol-X's instance, full-strength Titanium) rocket engine to be compatible with nanosatellites like CubeSats. We talked about what 3D printing means for the future of space-tech, Sol-X's other exciting projects like their first product, the Gravity Development Board" and their Space Diving Suit.

The following is the raw transcript from our chat:
